# Compatibility Bridging

Computica's Faction Forks includes compatibility bridging for selected non-faction Starsector mods.

## Current Targets

- Ashes of The Domain
- Industrial.Evolution
- Nexerelin
- Star Lords

## Possible Work Areas

- Faction integration fixes
- Market and industry adjustments
- Diplomacy and invasion behavior review
- Campaign-layer balance checks
- Economy and production compatibility passes
- Correction of broken or outdated mod interactions
- Optional support where the original mod never had it

## Compatibility Policy

Compatibility support varies per fork. Some factions may only need basic cleanup. Others may require deeper campaign-side work.

Support does not mean every possible mod combination is guaranteed. The goal is practical stability, cleaner behavior, and better integration with commonly used mod setups.
